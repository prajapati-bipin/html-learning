HTML List Examples

Unordered List (<ul>)

Use when order doesn’t matter:

  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Cherries
  • Dates

Ordered List (<ol>)

Use when sequence matters:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and water
  3. Bake for 30 minutes.
  4. Let cool before serving.

Nested Lists

You can nest lists for sub-items:

  • Fruits
    1. Apple
    2. Banana
    3. Cherry
  • Vegetables
    1. Carrot
    2. Broccoli
    3. Spinach
